### 1) My Bot keeps Getting Deleted
<P3>
Follow this tutorial about whitelisting apps or folders :-
<br />
<br />
Whitelisting an App on Antivirus Software
=========================================

1. Windows Defender (Microsoft Defender)
----------------------------------------
1. Open "Windows Security" (search in Start menu).
2. Go to "Virus & threat protection".
3. Click "Manage settings" under Virus & threat protection settings.
4. Scroll to "Exclusions" and click "Add or remove exclusions".
5. Click "Add an exclusion" → choose File, Folder, or Process, and select your app.
<br />
2. Avast/AVG
------------
1. Open the antivirus app.
2. Go to "Menu" → "Settings".
3. Find "Exceptions".
4. Click "Add Exception".
5. Browse to your app’s path or paste the file path manually.
<br />
3. Panda Dome
-------------
1. Open Panda Dome.
2. Go to "Settings" → "Antivirus".
3. Find the "Exclusions" section.
4. Add the path of your app or folder.
<br />
4. Bitdefender
--------------
1. Open Bitdefender.
2. Go to "Protection" → "Antivirus".
3. Click "Open" under Advanced Threat Defense.
4. Go to the "Settings" tab.
5. Add the app under "List of exceptions".
<br />
5. Kaspersky
------------
1. Open Kaspersky.
2. Go to "Settings" → "Threats and Exclusions".
3. Click "Manage exclusions".
4. Add your app’s path and optionally mark it as trusted.
<br />
6. Norton
---------
1. Open Norton.
2. Go to "Settings" → "Antivirus".
3. Click the "Scans and Risks" tab.
4. Scroll to "Exclusions / Low Risks".
5. Add files or folders to both Items to Exclude from Scans and Items to Exclude from Auto-Protect.
</P3>
